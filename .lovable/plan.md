

# The Salad House - One Page Website

A modern, minimal one-page website that puts your delicious menu front and center.

## Design Style
- **Modern & Minimal aesthetic** with clean white backgrounds, plenty of whitespace, and bold typography
- Fresh green accent colors to evoke health and freshness
- Elegant sans-serif fonts for a contemporary restaurant feel

## Page Sections

### 1. Hero Section
- Large, impactful header with "The Salad House" branding
- Tagline emphasizing fresh, healthy food
- Smooth scroll down to the menu

### 2. Menu Section (Main Focus)
The menu will be organized into clear categories for easy browsing:

- **Rice Bowls** (Mexican themed bowls - ranging from ₹250-399)
- **Salad Bowls** (Falafel, Paneer Tikka, Nachos bowls - ₹329)
- **Yogurt & Fruit Bowls** (Fresh fruit, chocolate, protein options - ₹299-549)
- **Wraps** (Paneer Tikka, Peri Peri Potato - ₹250-329)
- **Sides & Bites** (Peri Peri Potato Bites - ₹249)
- **Beverages** (Fresh juices, milkshakes, sodas - ₹40-249)
- **Dips & Extras** (Guacamole, Salsa, Sour Cream, Tzatziki, Corn Salsa - ₹129-599)

Each item will display the name, description (where applicable), and price in a clean card layout.

### 3. Location Section
- Embedded Google Maps showing your restaurant location
- Address and contact details displayed alongside the map
- Operating hours (if you'd like to provide them)

## Technical Notes
- Fully responsive design (works on mobile, tablet, and desktop)
- Smooth scrolling between sections
- No backend needed - this is a simple informational website
